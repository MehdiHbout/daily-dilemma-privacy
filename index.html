Daily Dilemma Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Daily Dilemma respects your privacy.

Daily Dilemma does not collect personal data.

The app does not require a user account, does not use a backend server, does not use analytics, and does not share user data with third parties.

Local Data

The following data may be stored locally on your device:

  • votes
  • favorites
  • vote history
  • language preferences
  • streaks
  • achievements
  • daily reminder preferences

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us or to third parties.

Notifications

Daily Dilemma may request notification permission only to send optional daily reminders. You can accept, refuse, or disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

Ads and In-App Purchases

Daily Dilemma does not currently contain ads or in-app purchases.

If ads or in-app purchases are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
